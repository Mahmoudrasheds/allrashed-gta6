The criminals, conspirators, and power brokers shaping Leonida's underworld.

Protagonist

Lucia

The lead protagonist and the driving force behind much of GTA 6's narrative. Described as sharp, calculating, and fiercely loyal to those she trusts. Her story arc intertwines romance, crime, and survival in a modern Vice City underbelly.

Raised in Leonida and hardened by a series of setbacks early in life, Lucia enters the story fresh off a stint that taught her how to read people, talk her way out of trouble, and pull a trigger when talking fails. Her partnership with Jason starts as a means of survival and evolves into the emotional core of the game.
